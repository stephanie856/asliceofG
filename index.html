About A Slice of G

Our Story

A Slice of G was born from a passion for combining traditional Caribbean flavors with artisan baking techniques. Every cake and cookie we create is a labor of love, crafted with premium ingredients and infused with carefully selected rums.

We believe that every celebration deserves something special, something that creates lasting memories. That's why we pour our heart into every batch, ensuring that each bite delivers not just incredible taste, but an experience.

From our kitchen to your table, we're committed to quality, authenticity, and the joy that comes from sharing exceptional baked goods with the people you love.
